Privacy Policy for GratitudeLens

Last Updated: November 23, 2025
Effective Date: November 23, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to GratitudeLens. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our iOS app.

Key Privacy Promise: GratitudeLens is designed with privacy-first architecture. Your gratitude entries, photos, and voice recordings stay in your personal iCloud account and are never stored on our servers.

Information We Collect

1. User-Generated Content (Stored in Your iCloud)

When you use GratitudeLens, the following information is stored in your personal iCloud account (not on our servers):

  • Gratitude entries (text, voice transcriptions, or photo descriptions)
  • Photos you capture or select from your library
  • Voice recordings and their transcriptions
  • AI-generated reflections based on your entries
  • Entry metadata (date, time, entry type)

Storage Location: All this data is stored in your iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service. We cannot access, view, or modify your entries.

2. Information Processed for AI Reflections

When you create an entry, the following information is temporarily sent to our secure server (CloudFlare Worker) for AI processing:

  • Your entry content (text, photo, or voice transcription)
  • Device identifier (anonymized, used for rate limiting only)

This information is:

  • Sent securely via HTTPS encryption
  • Processed by OpenAI's GPT API to generate your AI reflection
  • Not stored on our servers after processing
  • Not retained by OpenAI (per OpenAI's API terms, data is deleted after 30 days and not used for training)

3. Technical Information

We do NOT collect:

  • ❌ Your name or email address (unless you contact support)
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Device information beyond anonymized rate-limiting IDs
  • ❌ Analytics or tracking data
  • ❌ Advertising IDs
  • ❌ Contacts or other personal information

How We Use Your Information

AI-Generated Reflections

  • Your entry content is sent to OpenAI's API to generate personalized gratitude reflections
  • Content is moderated for safety using OpenAI's Moderation API (free and automatic)
  • AI processing happens in real-time; content is not stored after generation

Content Moderation

  • User content is automatically screened using OpenAI's Moderation API to detect:
    • Violence, hate speech, or harassment
    • Self-harm or crisis indicators
    • Inappropriate or harmful content
  • If content is flagged, we log the category and timestamp (NOT the content itself) for safety monitoring
  • Moderation logs are automatically deleted after 30 days

Rate Limiting

  • Device identifiers are used to prevent abuse (limit: 10 requests per minute)
  • This protects our service and keeps costs manageable for free users

Data Storage & Security

iCloud Sync

  • Your entries are stored in your private iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit
  • Data is encrypted by Apple both in transit and at rest
  • Only you can access your entries across your Apple devices
  • We cannot access your iCloud data

Our Server Infrastructure

  • CloudFlare Workers process AI requests (serverless, ephemeral)
  • No persistent storage of your content on our servers
  • HTTPS encryption for all API communication
  • API keys secured as environment variables (never in app code)

Third-Party Services

OpenAI (AI Processing):

  • Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
  • Terms: https://openai.com/policies/terms-of-use
  • Data retention: 30 days for API requests, then deleted
  • Not used for training AI models

Apple iCloud (Data Storage):

  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
  • Your iCloud data is subject to Apple's privacy terms
  • End-to-end encrypted (we cannot access it)

Your Privacy Rights

Access & Export

  • All your data is in your iCloud account
  • Export your entries as PDF, Markdown, or JSON from the Profile → Export screen
  • You own your data completely

Deletion

  • Delete individual entries:
    • Long-press (hold) any entry in History → Tap "Delete" in menu
    • Or open entry detail → Tap menu (•••) → "Delete" → Confirm
  • Delete all entries: Profile → "Delete All Entries" (requires Face ID/Touch ID confirmation)
  • Deletion is permanent and removes data from iCloud immediately

Opt-Out of AI Processing

  • GratitudeLens requires AI processing to function
  • If you prefer not to use AI features, we recommend a non-AI journaling app

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

  • GratitudeLens is intended for users aged 13 and older
  • We display an age gate on first launch requiring users to confirm they are 13+
  • We do NOT knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • If we learn that a user is under 13, we will delete their account
  • For parents: If your child is using this app, please supervise their usage and review this Privacy Policy

COPPA Rights: If you believe your child under 13 has used this app, please contact us at support@gratitudelens.com to delete their data.

Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period
Gratitude entries (iCloud) Until you delete them
AI reflections (iCloud) Until you delete them
API requests (OpenAI) 30 days, then deleted by OpenAI
Moderation logs 30 days, then auto-deleted
Rate limit data Resets every 60 seconds

International Users (GDPR/CCPA)

GDPR Rights (European Users)

  • Right to access: Export your data via Profile → Export
  • Right to erasure: Delete data via app settings
  • Right to data portability: Export as JSON
  • Right to restrict processing: Not applicable (processing is essential to service)
  • Right to object: You may stop using the app and delete your data

CCPA Rights (California Users)

  • Right to know: This Privacy Policy discloses what data we collect
  • Right to delete: Delete data via app settings
  • Right to opt-out of sale: We do NOT sell your personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination: Exercising your rights won't affect your service

To exercise your rights: Email support@gratitudelens.com with your request.

Content Safety & Reporting

  • We use OpenAI's Moderation API to screen content for safety
  • Harmful content (violence, self-harm, hate) is blocked with appropriate resources (988 Lifeline, Crisis Text Line)
  • Users can report inappropriate AI responses via the "Report" button on reflection cards
  • Reports are sent to support@gratitudelens.com and reviewed within 24 hours

Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • Changes will be posted in the app with an updated "Last Updated" date
  • Significant changes will be announced via in-app notification
  • Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance
